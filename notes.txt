Climate Modeling using AI:
AI enhances climate modeling by integrating massive datasets and predicting complex environmental patterns.
It improves accuracy in forecasting extreme events and supports sustainable policy decisions.

Deep Learning in Lung Cancer Detection:
Deep learning models analyze medical images to identify subtle cancerous patterns often missed by humans.
They enable early diagnosis, improving patient outcomes and supporting precision medicine.

 Deepfake in Fake News Detection:
AI systems detect deepfakes by analyzing inconsistencies in audio, video, and facial features.
This helps curb misinformation, safeguarding media integrity and public trust.
